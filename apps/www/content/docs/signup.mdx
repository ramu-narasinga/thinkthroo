# Sign Up

Create a ThinkThroo account to access documentation, projects, and learning resources.

## Steps

1. Click the **Sign Up** button in the top-right corner of the website.
2. Enter your **Full Name**.
3. Enter a valid **Email Address**.
4. Create a secure **Password**.
5. Confirm your password.
6. Click **Create Account**.
7. Verify your email address if verification is required.
8. Once verified, log in to your account.

## Troubleshooting

### Email already exists
If you receive an error that the email is already registered, try logging in instead.

### Didn't receive verification email
Check your spam folder or request a new verification email.
