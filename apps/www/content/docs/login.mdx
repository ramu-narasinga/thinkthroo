# Login

Use your ThinkThroo account credentials to access the platform.

## Steps

1. Click the **Login** button in the top-right corner of the website.
2. Enter your registered **Email Address**.
3. Enter your **Password**.
4. Click **Login**.
5. You will be redirected to your dashboard after successful authentication.

## Forgot Password

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Check your email for a password reset link.
4. Create a new password and log in again.

## Troubleshooting

### Invalid email or password
Verify that your credentials are correct and try again.

### Account locked
Contact support if your account remains locked after multiple attempts.
